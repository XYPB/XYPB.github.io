Bio

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Yale University, major in BME, advised by Prof. Nicha C. Dvornek. I am also actively collaborating with Prof. John Onofrey. Before that, I got my Bachelor's degree in CS at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, advised by Prof. Andrew Owens, and another Bachelor's degree in ECE at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

My research interest lies in the computer vision, multi-modality learning, and medical image analysis. I am always thrilled to work on the questions that address real-world needs.

I am also honored to serve as a reviewer in top-tier conferences and journals, including CVPR, NeurIPS, AAAI, MICCAI, ISBI, ICLR, AISTATS, and IEEE-TMM.

I am now actively looking for full-time internship opportunity starting from 2025 summer. Please contact me if you have any openings!
